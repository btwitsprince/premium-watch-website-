Since 1884

A House Built on
Obsession

1884

The Founding

Édouard Aure establishes a small atelier on Rue du Rhône, Geneva, with twelve artisans and a singular obsession: perfection of the escapement.

1923

The Tourbillon Revolution

Aureveil patents the first dual-axis tourbillon, eliminating gravitational variance across all orientations — a century ahead of its time.

1967

Royal Appointments

Appointed as official watchmaker to three European royal houses. The Chrono I becomes the most sought-after diplomatic gift of the era.

2019

The Digital renaissance

The Aureveil AI Concierge launches — the world's first artificial intelligence applied to haute horlogerie curation and personalization.

2026

Collection VI

Our most ambitious chapter yet: three ultra-limited editions that merge 142 years of heritage with the precision of tomorrow.
